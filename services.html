Services The Children we provide services...

Any child (birth to 18 years of age), who is facing developmental challenges (Physical, Social-emotional, academic and behavior ), can be seen at the Vikash & Vanivikash “Children’s Rehab Centre” (CRC).

The Children we provide services

Vikash & Vanivikash “Children’s Rehab Centre”(CRC) provides Developmental Therapeutic, Training & Education to facilitate:-
 
(Attention, Eye contact, imitation, concentration, comprehension, memory, thinking, reasoning, coordination, reading, writing, spelling, listening, speaking, math and many more)
 

  • Developing Motor / Movement skills.
  • Developing Sensory - Perceptual skills.
  • Facilitating Speech-Language and Communication skills.
  • Fostering Cognitive, thinking skills.
  • Developing Social-Emotional skills.
  • Developing Self - help skills,
  • Encouraging Self –Esteem and Self- Control.
  • Managing Problem Behaviors and 
  • Developing Academic performance.

 

Psychological counseling for both parents and child are also available here.
