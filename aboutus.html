Aboutus We are into...

 “VIKASH & VANIVIKASH” Children’s Rehab Centre (CRC), is a “Developmental Therapeutic, Training and Education Centre” providing individualized (one to one) services for children with developmental challenges (Problem in movement, co-ordination, learning, speech-language, social-emotional & behavior ) in and around Rourkela,  Odisha, India,  since 2003 .

Some children  are slow/backward/different due to immature of age  appropriate developmental skills, The child is slow in development means he/she is more or less slow in one or many aspects ; somatic (body), Mental , Perceptual and motor. Some children are temporarily backward in development but otherwise normal or some may be severely backward. All children are not alike, Individual differences are related to the degree of organization and development of body and mind.

As parents you will have concerns & hopes for your child. If your child is not developing like their typical peers, you are likely to be all the more anxious & worried. Vikash & Vanivikash has been providing comprehensive services  using variety of inovative training and teaching techniques to improve each child’s success with communication, peer activities, fine/gross motor and self - help skills.

Developmental Therapeutic Training & Education

iIdentifying a child with ADD/ADHD, PDD/ Autism, Mental Retardation, Learning Disability or other developmental disabilities and placing them in a special school/special class in a normal school is not the part of our services. The aim is to make them to move well, speak fluently, play and develop the skill necessary for everyday learning and living. Over all make them to physically, emotionally and functionally well organized person in the society. It does not say anything about the cure of your children but develops their optimal potentiality in thinking, learning, living and moving.

Our staff
Our staff are highly educated, and credential Professional (registered professional “Rehabilitation Council of India”, New Delhi), provided services since 2003. They have excellent performance and more than 12 years of experience in handling the child with Physical, emotional and mental health problem with many successes in educating and integrating them into normal school.
